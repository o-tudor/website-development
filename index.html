WHY STUDY IN INFORMATION & COMMUNICATIONS?

Here at Digital Student, we are all students studying within the Information & Communications department.

Because of the diversity that the department has in terms of course route options means that we can work collaboratively across different routes to produce the website that you a currently reading from now. What this means for you as a student is that you get to work on a great verity of projects, with different people who have different skill sets, this teamwork is immensely valuable in developing your professional communication skills and project management as well as specific subject skills that you wish to focus on. This will not only make you stand out to potential employers but will make you and all-round better person.
