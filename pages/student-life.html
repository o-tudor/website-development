Student Life

Being a student is considered by some to the best time of your life! So don’t forget to enjoy it. One of the best things about studying within the Information & Communications department, besides the versatility and great career opportunities, is the fact that you are entering into a very new, up and coming community of like minded people, that all love to talk and share ideas, there are so many events and meetups happening around manchester it's hard to know where to start.
