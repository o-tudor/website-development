Post Graduation

Post-degree prospects

Manchester Metropolitan students can access careers support throughout their studies and after graduation in a variety of ways including faculty drop-in services, e-guidance, employer events and careers appointments. The University has had more than 10,000 students choose to study for a post-graduate qualification. More than 70 professional bodies and associations work in partnership with the University to provide the right kind of courses for the private and public sectors. Most post-graduate degrees are taught over the course of 1 year but there is an option to study part-time.

Post-grad jobs

Graduate opportunities are advertised in a variety of ways; via careers fairs, graduate job websites, company websites, recruitment agencies and social media. Within the Digital industry, Manchester is the perfect place to be at this time of development. The university is in close proximity to BBC Media City and hundreds of digital agencies within the city.
