Freshers Week

Your first week as a university student! It's going to be full of late nights, meeting new friends, and most importantly having lots of fun. Here are some helpful tips and useful websites to get you through freshers week.

Night's out

There will be many different events on each night so a great place to see all of the events in one place is the skiddle website, You can also purchase tickets for events on skiddle, however it's a good idea to wait until you meet your flat mates and all decide on events together.

Visit Skiddle.com ›

Socials and Sports Clubs

Along with nights out freshers week also offers the opportunity for you to sign up to socials and sports clubs. This is possible at freshers fairs which you will find information about on the student's union website.

Get involved here ›

Student Discount Events

Freshers week will also allow you to grab some bargains with the ‘MCR Student Night Out’ at Manchester's Arndale shopping centre. This night will allow you to get discounts on over 200 stores (Make sure you sign up to unidays and download the app). Best of all you can also get yourself a dominoes pizza for just £1.00!
