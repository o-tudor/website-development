Tech Social

Tech socials are a great way to meet new friends that are interested in the same subjects as you as well as a brilliant opportunity to start expanding your professional network. Around Manchester there are loads of ways to get involved, with the chance to meet new people at each one. Some even offer free food and drink. Click on the headings below to explore different events and meetups.

Norther User Experiance (Better know as NUX) ›

User experience or UX is something that is talked about across the entire digital industry and is an ever evolving topic of conversation. If you don’t know anything about it right now, don’t worry! There will be plenty of time to learn and discover, whilst you are here.

NUX are great regular meet-up sessions to learn from and talk to enthusiastic people in a friendly atmosphere that are working in the UX/UI sector of the digital industry. With guest speakers talking about the latest trends, UX/UI networking (and free beer & pizza), these are worthwhile events to attend to get ahead in the industry or just to meet great people. These sessions are usually free to attend and tickets can be bought online, but hurry, as they sell out quickly. NUX also organises conferences throughout the year in Manchester and Leeds. These are usually paid ticket events but are discounted for students.

Meet Up ›

Meet Up is a great website to use to learn about different social activities that happen across the city. From nights out to organized hikes, there is something for everyone. There are plenty of people to go to these events alone, as it is a good way to meet people and make new friends with individuals who share your interests. The site can also be used to discover professional meet ups where there will be people from your chosen industry holding discussions. These are often used for networking and are a great way to get your foot in the door early on in your career.

Hackathons ›

Hackathons take place all around the city, including the Manchester City football stadium. These events are organised for a variety of different reasons but all focus on generating new and exciting ideas for businesses. The hackathons help to contrast opinions and build improvements for organisations. They are also a great way to learn new skills within the digital industry, as well as gather the experience of research ready for when you go into a creative job.
